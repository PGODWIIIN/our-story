Our Story
Since 1986

KayandKompany Organic Hair & Beauty Salon - Founded in May 1986 by Kay Eracleous

Kay trained and worked for Vidal Sassoon including, other various top west-end salons. Her dream was to bring a quality, high class, and an affordable boutique hair salon to her local North London, Muswell Hill area.

With Kays wealth of knowledge, expertise and passion in all aspects of hairdressing including, customer care, and a bank loan! KayandKompany salon was born.

The first five years of opening were hectic and extremely hard work, especially with two young children and a Greek husband to look after. Including, a bank loan to pay off!

Luckily for me, I had my dear Greek Cypriot mama who helped me achieve my dreams and goals by caring for my two daughters (including the husband!) and doing all my housework!

Sadly, my beloved mama passed away at the age of 86 in 2018 and I'll never, ever stop missing her...

I honestly believe that dreams happen if you are passionate about something and prepared to work 24/7 to achieve them. Plus, I had a family, friends and clients who believed in me and I couldnt let them down.
However, entrepreneurship, running a business and leadership does not suit everyone, and in my book, thats ok too. Whatever inspires you and makes you happy is good.

We invest in our hairdressing team with quality on-going training; our great team have helped me, in making the business, and our Grace Hair&Beauty salon brand a huge success.

"You don't build a business; you build people, a great team and together, you build the business".

The individual client approach is one of our secrets for our excellent, 'good salon' reputation, and our success.

Our aim, to make others look and feel fabulous and for us, it is incredibly, satisfying and rewarding.

Love what I do, my job, my work, my clients and my colleagues - Being a hairdresser is also one of my life hobbies! - Kay Eracleous X

P.S. One of my beautiful baby girls Ellena Eracleous (who was only one year old when I opened the salon)grew-up to be an amazing hairdresser too! Proud to say, in 2010 I made Elli a KayandKompany salon partner and our story will grow and continue...

Our Salon's Guarantees

Our Grace Hair&Beauty salon carry a seven-day *'Satisfaction' guarantee on haircuts, hair colours and perms.
Our guarantee allows our clients seven days to decide if they are happy with their hair. If you are not satisfied with your hair, then please notify us within seven days.
We will arrange for you to come back at a time (up to 4 weeks) that is suitable and convenient for you. We will then free of charge, happily re-do your hair.

*Please Note: Our seven-day *Satisfaction guarantee policy is NOT validfor 'money back' or 'money refund'.
